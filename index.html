HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Objective

Answer as many questions as possible correctly and as quickly as you can!

⏱️ Timer

You have 3-5 seconds to answer each question. The faster you answer, the more bonus points you get!

🔥 Combo System

Answer correctly to build your combo multiplier (x2, x3, x4, etc.). Wrong answers reset your combo!

⚡ Reflex Bonus

Answer in less than 1 second to get a reflex bonus and extra points!

🎯 Scoring

Base points + Combo multiplier + Reflex bonus = Your score for that question!
